Product Research for Multiple Stores

⏱ 15 min Research Lesson 9 of 23

The MEO Product Research Difference

Product research for a single store is about finding one winning product. Product research for MEO is about finding categories and supplier relationships that can power multiple stores.

The Three Product Criteria for MEO

  • Multi-Platform Viability — Can this product sell on Amazon AND Shopify AND Etsy without major adaptation?
  • Supplier Depth — Can the supplier support 3x volume? Do they have adjacent products for future stores?
  • Niche Adjacency — Does this product open a niche where you can build 2-3 related stores?

Research Tools Stack

  • Helium 10 / Jungle Scout — Amazon search volume, competition, revenue estimates
  • EverBee — Etsy market research
  • Minea / AdSpy — Spy on winning Facebook and TikTok ads to find hot products
  • Google Trends — Seasonality and trend direction for any category
  • Alibaba / 1688 — Source and verify supplier depth and MOQ flexibility
MEO Research Habit: When you find a winning product, immediately research 5-10 adjacent products in the same niche. These become your second store's catalog before you even open it.

Avoiding Common Research Mistakes

Don't chase trending products with no sustainable demand. Don't pick products that only work on one platform. And never rely on a single supplier for a product that anchors multiple stores — dual-source everything from day one.
