Automation Tools for MEO

⏱ 16 min Tools Lesson 18 of 23

The MEO Automation Stack

Automation is the force multiplier that makes MEO possible. The right tools can eliminate 60-70% of repetitive manual tasks across your stores, freeing you for strategy, supplier relationships, and growth decisions.

Core Automation Categories

  • Pricing Automation — Auto-repricer tools keep you competitive without manual monitoring (Amazon: BQool, Repricer Express | Shopify: Bold Pricing)
  • Order Management — Auto-route orders to correct 3PL or supplier based on SKU/channel (Linnworks, Skubana)
  • Customer Service — AI-powered helpdesk auto-resolves 40-60% of tickets without human touch (Gorgias + AI, Re:amaze)
  • Review Requests — Automated post-purchase review request sequences (FeedbackWhiz for Amazon)
  • Reporting — Automated P&L reports pulled from all stores into one dashboard (Sellerboard, BeProfit)

Zapier / Make for Cross-Platform Automation

Zapier and Make (formerly Integromat) let you connect tools that don't natively integrate. MEO use cases: When a new order appears in Shopify → create a task in ClickUp for your VA → send a Slack notification. When Amazon inventory drops below threshold → automatically email your supplier with a reorder request.

🤖 Automation Priority Order

Automate in this order: (1) Repetitive — same task done identically every time. (2) Time-sensitive — tasks that need to happen fast. (3) Error-prone — tasks where human error causes downstream problems. Strategic decisions should stay human.
