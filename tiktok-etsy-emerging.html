Amazon FBA for MEO

⏱ 18 min Platform Deep-Dive Lesson 6 of 23

Why Amazon is Often Your Anchor Platform

Amazon generates over 40% of all US e-commerce sales. For most MEO operators, it's the highest-volume, lowest-friction sales channel when set up correctly. FBA means Amazon warehouses, picks, packs, and ships your products — and handles returns.

Amazon FBA MEO Strategy

  • Separate Seller Accounts — Structure your LLCs so each entity can have its own Amazon account per ToS.
  • ASIN Management — Manage in bulk using tools like Helium 10 or Jungle Scout.
  • Brand Registry — Register each store's brand separately for full content control and counterfeit protection.
  • Inventory Distribution — Amazon distributes FBA inventory across warehouses. Plan for this in cash flow.
⚠️ FBA Cost Reality

FBA fees typically run 25-35% of your selling price once you include referral fees, FBA fulfillment fees, and storage. Make sure your product margins support this before expanding on Amazon.

Amazon-Specific MEO Risks

Amazon account suspensions can kill a store overnight. MEO operators must maintain strict policy compliance on every Amazon store and never share inventory, seller information, or IP addresses across accounts without proper entity separation.

Key Amazon MEO Tools

  • Helium 10 / Jungle Scout — Product research and keyword ranking
  • Repricer Express / BQool — Automated pricing across ASINs
  • SellerBoard / Sellerapp — Per-store profitability analytics
  • RestockPro — Inventory reorder automation
