Hiring VAs and Building Your MEO Team

⏱ 15 min Team Lesson 19 of 23

The MEO Team Architecture

Successful MEO operations run on lean teams of specialists — not full-time employees in every role. The typical progression: solo operator → first VA → VA team → specialist contractors → operations manager.

Your First Hire: The E-commerce VA

Your first Virtual Assistant should handle: listing management, order issue resolution, supplier communication, basic customer service, and reporting. Budget: $5-15/hour depending on experience and location (Philippines, Eastern Europe, Latin America are most popular).

Where to Find Great E-commerce VAs

  • OnlineJobs.ph — Large pool of experienced Filipino VAs, many with e-commerce background
  • Upwork — Global talent, higher cost, more vetting infrastructure
  • Fiverr Business — Project-based specialists for specific tasks
Hiring Rule: Never hire for a role that doesn't yet have an SOP. You're hiring someone to follow your documented system. SOPs come first, then hiring.

The MEO Specialist Roles (As You Scale)

  • PPC Specialist — Manages Amazon and/or Meta ads across all stores
  • Customer Service Manager — Owns helpdesk across all channels
  • Inventory/Ops Coordinator — Manages 3PL relationships, reorders, and stock levels
  • Operations Manager — Oversees VAs and day-to-day operations when you have 5+ stores
