Financial Dashboards for MEO

⏱ 14 min Finance Lesson 21 of 23

Flying Blind vs. Flying with Data

Most e-commerce operators confuse revenue with profit. In MEO, this is catastrophic — you can be generating $500K in revenue across 4 stores while actually losing money. A real-time financial dashboard per store is not optional.

The 5 Numbers Every Store Needs Weekly

  • Gross Revenue — Total sales before returns and fees
  • Net Revenue — After returns, platform fees, and discounts
  • COGS — Cost of goods + shipping to warehouse + packaging
  • Gross Profit — Net Revenue minus COGS
  • Net Profit — Gross Profit minus ads, team, software, overhead

MEO Dashboard Tools

  • Sellerboard — Best per-store P&L for Amazon sellers
  • BeProfit — Multi-store Shopify profit dashboard
  • Triple Whale — Premium attribution + profit tool for Shopify/Meta
  • Google Sheets / Notion — Manual but fully custom consolidated MEO dashboard
Financial Rule: Review per-store P&L weekly. Review consolidated MEO P&L monthly. Any store that's been unprofitable for 3 consecutive months needs a transformation plan or an exit plan.

Cash Flow in MEO

Cash flow is tighter in MEO because you're funding inventory, ads, and operations across multiple fronts simultaneously. Amazon pays every 2 weeks; Shopify pays daily. Build a 90-day cash reserve per active store before opening a new one.
