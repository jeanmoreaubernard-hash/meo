TikTok Shop, Etsy & Emerging Channels

⏱ 13 min Platform Deep-Dive Lesson 8 of 23

TikTok Shop: The New Frontier

TikTok Shop is growing faster than any e-commerce platform in history. In 2023-2024, it surpassed $20 billion in GMV globally, driven by creator-led commerce.

  • Affiliate Creator Program — Your products can be promoted by thousands of creators with zero upfront cost (commission only)
  • LIVE Shopping — Real-time selling via livestream, averaging 10x conversion vs. standard listings
  • Shop Ads — Paid ads that blend seamlessly with organic content

Etsy: The Handmade & Niche Marketplace

Etsy's 90+ million active buyers are specifically seeking unique, artisan, vintage, or personalized products. For MEO operators with print-on-demand, handmade, or niche product stores, Etsy can deliver consistent passive sales with minimal ad spend.

  • Low competition for specific search terms vs. Amazon
  • Buyers willing to pay premium for uniqueness
  • Integrates seamlessly with Printful, Printify, and Gelato for POD

Walmart Marketplace

Walmart's marketplace is invite-only but less competitive than Amazon for many categories. Walmart's fulfillment service (WFS) mirrors FBA's model. For established MEO operators, Walmart often delivers 20-30% additional revenue with minimal incremental effort.

🎯 Channel Priority Guide

Start: Amazon + Shopify → Add: Etsy (if niche fits) or TikTok Shop (if product is visual) → Advanced: Walmart Marketplace.
